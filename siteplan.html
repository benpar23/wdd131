WDD 131 Site Plan - Ben Parada

Site Name

The Site Name will be Poke-Experts as it relates to "expert" knowledge for Competitive Pokemon.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to help aspiring and current competitive Pokemon players find information easily. Newer players can discover how to play and be introduced to information that will guide them as they begin. More seasoned players will be able to access information to keep them up to date with current trends.

Scenarios

How do I play Pokemon competitively?
What strategies should I use?
What Pokemon should I be using?
