Welcome to Poke-Experts!

Here you will find useful information on getting started or getting better with competitive Pokemon, also known as VGC. As players and lovers of Pokemon ourselves, we wanted to create a space where aspiring Pokemon Masters and reigning Champions alike could find resources to further their journey into Competitive VGC.

What is Competitive VGC?

Competitive Pokemon, or more commonly Competitive VGC, is the official competitive circuit of Pokemon worldwide. Ranging from local to regional/national championships all the way to Global championships, the formats and rulesets are catered to a fun yet challenging way to play Pokemon.
